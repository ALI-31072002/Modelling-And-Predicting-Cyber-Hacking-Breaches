Love of life

Mirai has been designed to encourage and preserve our love for the natural world. It maximises the usage of open spaces, and connects us to the elements that surround us.

The Earth Element Ties that matter

Natural materials like wood and stone remind us of our connection to the earth, and help us feel secure.

The Fire Element Tend the fire within

Clerestory windows, reflective materials, sky lights, and glass facades invite warmth and light into the home, and uplifts the spirit.

The Air Element Freedom isn’t just another word

Proper positioning of windows, and powerful turbo ventilators enhance air circulation. Such focus on ventilation ensures that you breathe in the freshest of air.

The Water Element Wash away the dust

Water bodies, fountains, wetlands, and aquariums, soothe and heal body, mind and soul.

The Space Element See the bigger picture

Beautiful spaces, big views from windows, terraces, and gardens, remind us of the greatness of creation, and our place in it.
